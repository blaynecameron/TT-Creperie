Welcome to Tea Tree Crêperie!

Tea Tree Crêperie is a Cordon Bleu style eatery & the first of its kind in Jamaica. Though we specialize in international & herbal teas and incredible crêpes, we offer a tantalizing menu of classic breakfast, gluten-free as well as vegan delights, delicious desserts, quiche, salads, 100% Blue Mountain coffee drinks, blended and specialty drinks, top shelf offerings and many more hearty goodies by Chef Maree.
